How it works
STEP 01

Patterns checked
7 behavioral signals checked — balance drain, velocity, timing, amount.

STEP 04
Action taken
Transaction is allowed, flagged for review, or blocked — with a plain reason.
Key findings from PaySim
All fraud drains the sender's balance completely
In every single fraud case, the sender's balance becomes exactly zero. This is the strongest signal.
Only CASH_OUT and TRANSFER carry fraud
PAYMENT, DEBIT, and CASH_IN have zero fraud cases in PaySim. The model focuses on two risky types.
Fraud peaks between 1 AM and 5 AM
Late-night transactions carry much higher fraud probability — 41% of all fraud occurs in these hours.
